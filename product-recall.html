Product Recall

Phillips is recalling specific Bi-level PAP and CPAP devices. At AfzaSLEEPClinics, patient safety is of utmost concern, and we are fully mobilized to address this important situation. As authorized distributors, we are actively discussing the issue with Philips Respironics and waiting to receive specific instruction from Philips for the repair/replacement part.

We will be communicating with our patients as we receive more info & update our website notice as Philips releases details regarding repair/replacement process for its devices.

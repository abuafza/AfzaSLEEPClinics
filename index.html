AfzaSLEEPClinics is licensed by the Ontario Ministry of Health to provide home respiratory care under the Home Oxygen Program (HOP) and the Assistive Devices Program (ADP). Our main services are home oxygen therapy initiation and follow-up and PAP therapy initiation and follow-up. We also carry a variety of PAP therapy supplies and accessories from all major manufacturers. For referring physicians home oxygen assessments, overnight oximetry testing and PAP therapy download reports are available via referral and on request.

What is PAP?

PAP is an acronym for Positive Airway Pressure, a type of treatment for sleep apnea. The most common type of PAP therapy is CPAP or Continuous Positive Airway Pressure.
